Team 3543

C4 Robotics was founded in 2010 when a group of students came to Peter Cudmore, a physics teacher at Arnprior District High School, with the idea to create a team and participate in FIRST Logomotion.

Team 3543 (C4 Robotics) is now an FRC team, our mission is to inspire students and teachers to take part in engineering and science to prove that anyone can do anything once they set their minds to it.

Our 2017-2018 Sponsors

Every year our team needs to cover the costs of necessities like the competition entry fee, workshop equipment, and travel.
Building a robot costs a lot of money and without our generous sponsors we wouldn't be where we are today.
