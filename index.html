Team 3543

C4 Robotics was founded in 2010 when a group of students came to Peter Cudmore with the idea to create a team and participate in FIRST Logomotion.

Team 3543 (C4 Robotics) is now an FRC team from Arnprior District High School located in Arnprior, Ontario. Our mission, to inspire students and teachers to take part in engineering and science to prove that anyone can do anything once they set their minds to it.
